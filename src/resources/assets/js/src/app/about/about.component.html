About Email Tracker

Email tracker is a very simple system to track emails. It doesn't work on all providers*, but it's free to use, has no ads, and works most of the time.

Copy the image to the email you send, and when your recepient opens the email and loads this remote image, we'll save it as a visit and notify you. Click "new" below to generate a track.

* - some providers cache the images and the results can be inaccurate
